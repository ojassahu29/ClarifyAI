# Sample HR Policy Document

This document outlines the key policies and procedures for employees of our organization.

---

## 1. Remote Work Policy

### 1.1 Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible to work remotely up to **3 days per week** with prior manager approval.

### 1.2 Core Hours
Regardless of work location, all employees must be available during core hours: **10:00 AM to 3:00 PM** local time for meetings and collaboration.

### 1.3 Equipment
The company will provide a laptop and necessary software. Employees are responsible for maintaining a suitable home office environment with reliable internet connectivity.

### 1.4 Communication
Remote employees must be reachable via Slack and email during working hours. Response time expectations remain the same as in-office work.

---

## 2. Paid Time Off (PTO)

### 2.1 Annual Allowance
- **0-2 years of service:** 15 days per year
- **3-5 years of service:** 20 days per year  
- **6+ years of service:** 25 days per year

### 2.2 Carryover Policy
Employees may carry over up to **5 unused PTO days** to the following calendar year. Any unused days beyond 5 will be forfeited on December 31st.

### 2.3 Request Process
PTO requests should be submitted at least **2 weeks in advance** through the HR portal. Requests for more than 5 consecutive days require department head approval.

### 2.4 Blackout Periods
PTO may be restricted during peak business periods. These periods will be communicated at least 30 days in advance.

---

## 3. Code of Conduct

### 3.1 Professional Behavior
All employees are expected to maintain professional conduct in the workplace and during any company-related activities. This includes:
- Treating colleagues with respect and dignity
- Avoiding conflicts of interest
- Maintaining confidentiality of company information

### 3.2 Harassment and Discrimination
The company maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment and discrimination of any kind. All employees must complete annual training on these topics.

### 3.3 Reporting Violations
Violations should be reported to:
1. Your direct manager
2. Human Resources department
3. Anonymous ethics hotline: 1-800-XXX-XXXX

---

## 4. Performance Reviews

### 4.1 Review Schedule
Performance reviews are conducted **semi-annually** in January and July.

### 4.2 Process
1. Self-assessment submission
2. Manager evaluation
3. Peer feedback (optional)
4. Review meeting with manager
5. Goal setting for next period

### 4.3 Rating Scale
- **Exceeds Expectations:** Consistently performs above role requirements
- **Meets Expectations:** Fulfills all role requirements
- **Below Expectations:** Requires improvement in key areas

---

## 5. Expense Reimbursement

### 5.1 Eligible Expenses
- Business travel (flights, hotels, ground transportation)
- Client entertainment (with prior approval)
- Professional development and certifications
- Home office equipment (up to $500/year)

### 5.2 Submission Deadline
All expense reports must be submitted within **30 days** of the expense date. Reports submitted after 60 days may not be reimbursed.

### 5.3 Approval Limits
- Up to $500: Manager approval
- $500-$2000: Director approval
- Over $2000: VP approval required

---

## 6. Health and Safety

### 6.1 Workplace Safety
All employees must complete mandatory safety training within their first week of employment.

### 6.2 Incident Reporting
Any workplace accidents or near-misses must be reported to HR within **24 hours** using the incident report form.

### 6.3 Emergency Procedures
Familiarize yourself with emergency exits, first aid kit locations, and evacuation procedures for your work location.

---

## 7. Termination Procedures

### 7.1 Voluntary Resignation
Employees wishing to resign should provide at least **2 weeks written notice** to their manager and HR.

### 7.2 Exit Process
- Return all company property
- Complete knowledge transfer documentation
- Participate in exit interview (optional)
- Final paycheck will include unused PTO balance

### 7.3 Severance
Severance packages, if applicable, are determined on a case-by-case basis and require VP and HR approval.
